About Us


Meet the members of the Dunfermline Men's Shed team! Here, you can read about the faces you'll be seeing around the shed, and get to know what each member does.

Ron Skirving

Ron is our Chairman and keeps everything running smoothly. He also provides web and e-mail support. As a digital consultant, Ron advises on marketing the Dunfermline Men's Shed to the local community. He is also a Trustee of the Dunfermline Men's Shed charity.

Jim Leishman - Patron

Jim Leishman MBE is now in his third five year term of office as the Provost of the Kingdom of Fife, a remarkable achievement, and is Honorary Director of Dunfermline Athletic. We are honoured to have Jim as our Patron.

Mike Rowlinson

Mike is our Secretary and is responsible for recording of the minutes at Dunfermline Men's Shed meetings, He also handles communications including incoming calls and emails. Mike also helps with enewsletters, administration of our website and membership subscription. He is also a Trustee of the Dunfermline Men's Shed charity.
